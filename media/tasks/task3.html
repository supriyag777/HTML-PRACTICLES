Features of Badminton

Feature Description
Equipment Racket and shuttlecock
Players Singles (1 vs 1) or Doubles (2 vs 2)
Court Size 13.4 meters long and 6.1 meters wide
Net Height 1.55 meters at the center
Objective To hit the shuttlecock over the net and land it in the opponent's court without them returning it
Origin Originated in India as Poona, modern form developed in England
Olympic Status Part of the Olympics since 1992
Speed One of the fastest racket sports, shuttlecock can reach speeds over 200 mph
