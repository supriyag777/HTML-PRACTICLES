Different Types of Flowers

Simple HTML-only table listing several flower types with short descriptions and images. No CSS used.

Flower Description Image
Rose Classic garden flower known for its layered petals and wide range of colors; often symbolizes love and beauty.
Sunflower Tall plant with a large, bright yellow flower head that follows the sun; produces edible seeds.
Tulip Bulbous spring flower with smooth, cup-shaped blooms available in many vibrant colors.
Lily Fragrant, trumpet-shaped flowers that come in many varieties; popular in bouquets and gardens.
Orchid Delicate and diverse family of flowers, often grown as houseplants for their exotic blooms.
